Baldur's Gate

Description

Baldur's Gate 3 is a sprawling, story-rich RPG based on the Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition ruleset. It features deep character customization, tactical turn-based combat, and a branching narrative driven by player choices.

Story

Begins with you and others being captured by Mind Flayers, who implant you with tadpoles that will transform you into one of them. After a ship crash, you must race against time to find a cure, forming alliances and making difficult choices that shape the fate of the Forgotten Realms. The game explores themes of corruption, power, and the struggle against inevitable change.
