Wuthering Waves

Description

Wuthering Waves is an open-world action RPG set in a post-apocalyptic world devastated by a mysterious calamity known as the "Lament." Players explore a vibrant world, engage in fast-paced combat, and uncover the secrets of the Resonators.

Story

p>Players take on the role of Rover, who awakens with amnesia in a world recovering from the Lament. They must navigate the dangerous landscapes, encounter various factions, and discover their own connection to the Resonators, individuals with unique powers. The game explores themes of memory, survival, and the rebuilding of a shattered world.
