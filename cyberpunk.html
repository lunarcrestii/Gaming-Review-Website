Cyberpunk 2077

Description

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure RPG set in Night City, a megalopolis obsessed with power, glamour, and body modification.1 You play as V, a mercenary outlaw going after a one-of-a-kind implant that is the key to immortality.

Story

V's journey is a desperate struggle for survival in a corrupt and technologically advanced city. After a botched heist, V's mind is intertwined with the digital ghost of legendary rockerboy Johnny Silverhand. The game explores themes of identity, mortality, and the dangers of unchecked technological advancement, as V navigates the treacherous political landscape of Night City while fighting for their own existence.
